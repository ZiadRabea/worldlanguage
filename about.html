About World Language

Our History

Worldlang was born from a deep passion for technology and a desire to break down barriers in software development. Founded by Ziad Rabea, the project started from humble beginnings—an early fascination with science and the desire to solve problems through technology. From authoring a scientific paper at age 12 to building a 70,000-line social networking platform at 14, Ziad's journey has been defined by relentless curiosity and the pursuit of innovation. Joined by Soundous Bensakesli, a brilliant young scientist in her own right, we form a team driven by intuition and the shared goal of empowering others. Our vision is simple yet profound: to create a world where programming is accessible to everyone, regardless of language barriers. This led to the development of Worldlang, the first multilingual AI-powered programming language. Worldlang is more than just a tool—it is a reflection of our belief that anyone, with the right resources, can achieve more than they ever imagined. It was built with the intention not only to teach coding but to foster a global community of learners and innovators, encouraging them to become even better than we are.

Our Mission

At World Language, our mission is to bridge languages and minds. We believe that the power of coding should be accessible to all, regardless of language or background. Our goal is to simplify the world of programming, making it as intuitive as speaking your mother tongue. We're dedicated to breaking down barriers and empowering individuals to unleash their creativity through coding.

The World Language Experience

Imagine a world where programming is a seamless experience. World Language brings you that vision. With our innovative multilingual capabilities, you can write code in your preferred language effortlessly. Our built-in translator ensures that coding becomes a universal language. Say goodbye to syntax struggles, and welcome a world where coding is intuitive, accessible, and enjoyable.

Where AI Meets Imagination

World Language is where artificial intelligence merges with boundless imagination. Now you can write code in human language, eliminating the frustration of syntax errors. Whether you're prototyping a voice-controlled chatbot in two lines of code or creating a robotic masterpiece in four, World Language empowers you to turn your tech dreams into reality.

A Gateway to Creativity and Innovation

World Language is more than a programming language; it's a gateway to creativity and innovation. We thrive on shattering conventional barriers. Whether you're a budding engineer working on a graduation project or an enthusiast exploring new frontiers, World Language equips you with the tools to prototype and innovate without constraints. We're here to fuel your imagination and take programming to the next level.
